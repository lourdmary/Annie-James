How to Measure

Bust: Measure around the fullest part of your chest, keeping the measuring tape horizontal.

Waist: Measure around the narrowest part of your torso, typically just above your hip bones.

Hip: Measure around the fullest part of your hips, keeping the measuring tape horizontal.

Fitting Tips
  • Use a soft measuring tape
  • Measure over undergarments
  • Keep the tape snug but not tight
  • Measure at the fullest points
  • When in between sizes, size up for comfort
